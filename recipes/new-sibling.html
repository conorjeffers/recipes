Get a new little brther or sister!

Have you been waiting for a new little brother or sister to come along, only to have mum and dad be spoil sports? "We don't have money/space/time" are just excuses. Here's how you can help them make the right decision today!

Ingredients:

  • A pin.
  • One of dad's weird sticky balloons (still in the wrapper).

Steps:

  1. Firstly, make sure the coast is clear. You don't want mum and dad knowing what you're up to, so wait until they've popped to the shop or are cooking dinner.
  2. Take one of dad's sticky balloon wrappers out of the box without opening it.
  3. Take the pin and - very gently - poke a few holes through the wrapper and into the balloon. Be careful not to tear the wrapper, as this is a dead giveaway that someone has been up to no good.

Voila! Your new sibling should be on their way soon.
