Make your parents divorce!

Feel like mum and dad are a little too close? Ready to become another broken home statistic? Here's a few easy steps to get them separated, so you can be their number one focus again!

Ingredients:

  • Dad's coat, where he keeps his second phone.

Steps:

  1. Pretend to be "helping" put clothes away, and take dad's coat off the hanger.
  2. Handle the coat so that the phone slips out of dad's pocket.
  3. Allow mum to discover the existence of the second phone.

Another quick and easy one, but the impact will be huge!
