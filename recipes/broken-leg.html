A Broken Leg!

Fancy something quick and painful? Try setting up a broken leg for dad - he'll definitely appreciate the time off work!

Ingredients:

  • A banana peel, or something equally cartoonish to slip on.

Steps:

  1. Ask your parents if you can go and play in your room (you're not really playing, you're actually up to no good).
  2. Take the banana peel and place it near the middle of the stairs (not too high, or this would be a death recipe).
  3. Allow to sit until you a scream, crash and sobbing.

There you have it, mum or dad will likely now have a broken leg and be able to spend more time with you and less time at work!
